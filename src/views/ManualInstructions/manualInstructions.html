Import into Monarch

Follow these steps in your Monarch Money account to import each CSV file:

  1. a
    Go to Accounts → Add account
  2. b
    Choose Add manual account
  3. c
    Select your desired account type
  4. d
    Give it a name and starting balance of $0.00
  5. e
    Go to Edit → Upload transactions
  6. f
    Upload your account CSV file
  7. g
    Enable "Adjust account's balances based on these transactions"
  8. h
    Click Add to account
  9. i
    Repeat for all your accounts
