Frequently Asked Questions

Is my YNAB data really safe?

Absolutely. Your YNAB data is processed entirely in your browser using read-only API access. We have no servers to store your data, and we never see your transactions, account balances, or personal information. You can disconnect access at any time through YNAB's developer settings.

What data gets migrated?

We migrate your budgets, accounts, categories, payees, and transaction history. You have full control over what data to include—select specific accounts, date ranges, or budgets. The tool shows you exactly what will be exported before you confirm.

Do I need to pay for this tool?

The migration tool is completely free to use. If you find it valuable and want to support ongoing development and maintenance, you can optionally buy us a coffee. No payment is required to access any features.
