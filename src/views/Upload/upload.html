How does this work?

  1. Export your YNAB data by going to your YNAB budget settings and downloading the full export.
  2. Upload the YNAB ZIP file using the drag & drop area above or by clicking "Browse Files".
  3. Choose your import method - either manual CSV download or guided auto-import into Monarch Money.

100% Private: Your files are processed locally in your browser. Nothing is uploaded to our servers or stored externally.
