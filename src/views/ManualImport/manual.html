Manual Import

We’ve prepared your account files. Download your data and manually import them into Monarch Money.

How to manually import into Monarch

  1. Log into your Monarch account.
  2. Navigate to Accounts → Add Manual Account.
  3. Choose account type and set opening balance.
  4. Click "Upload Transactions" and select your downloaded CSV file.
  5. Repeat for each account.

Your files are processed locally and never uploaded externally.
