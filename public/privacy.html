2. Purpose and Data Use

We are completely transparent about how we use your data:

  • Primary Purpose: To transfer your transaction history, account information, and budget data from YNAB to Monarch Money as requested by you.
  • Secondary Purpose: To provide you with preview capabilities, account mapping tools, and migration status messaging to ensure accurate data transfer.
  • Token Management: We store YNAB-issued OAuth access and refresh tokens in secure HttpOnly cookies so we can make the YNAB API calls you request; these tokens are never shared with third parties and expire automatically (access tokens in ~1 hour, refresh tokens in up to 12 months).
  • No Other Uses: We do not use your data for analytics, marketing, advertising, or any other commercial purposes beyond the core functionality described above.

3. Data Collection and Handling

What Data We Access

Through the YNAB API, we access:

  • Your budget information and categories
  • Account names, types, and balances
  • Transaction history including payees, amounts, dates, and memos
  • Account-specific transaction counts and balances

How We Handle Your Data

  • Secure Processing: All data is processed securely using industry-standard encryption both in transit and at rest
  • Browser Processing: Budget, account, and transaction content stays in your browser memory/localStorage/sessionStorage and is never written to our databases because we do not operate any
  • Token Storage Only: The only data persisted outside your browser is the YNAB OAuth access/refresh tokens, which live in encrypted HttpOnly cookies on our Netlify functions solely so we can call the YNAB API on your behalf
  • Short-Lived Access Tokens: Access tokens automatically expire after approximately one hour; refresh tokens can be valid for up to one year but can be revoked at any time
  • Browser-Based Session: Session data is stored in your browser only and is automatically cleared when you complete the transfer, close the browser tab, or clear your browser data

Access Tokens and Security

  • We securely handle OAuth access tokens obtained directly from YNAB
  • Access tokens are encrypted and stored only for the duration of your active session
  • We never store your YNAB username, password, or any financial account credentials
  • All API communications use HTTPS encryption

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We guarantee that your data will not unknowingly be passed to any third party.

  • No Data Sales: We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal or financial data to any third parties
  • No Analytics Sharing: We do not share your data with analytics services, advertising networks, or data brokers
  • Intended Transfer Only: The only "sharing" of your data is the intended transfer to Monarch Money, which you explicitly authorize and initiate
  • Service Providers: We may use trusted service providers (like hosting services) who are bound by strict confidentiality agreements and only process data as necessary to provide our service

5. Data Storage and Management

  • Browser Storage: Budget, account, and transaction payloads are stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage/sessionStorage and are never uploaded to our servers
  • Session Data: Temporary transfer data is stored in browser sessionStorage and automatically cleared when you close the browser tab
  • Token Cookies: YNAB OAuth tokens are stored as HttpOnly Secure cookies managed by our Netlify functions; access tokens expire within one hour and refresh tokens within twelve months unless you revoke them earlier, and the in-app "Log Out" control immediately calls our revoke endpoint to clear both cookies
  • Retention Duration: Outside of those short-lived cookies, YNAB-derived budget data exists only during your active session and is purged when the transfer completes or when you close the tab
  • User Control: You have complete control over browser-stored data and can clear it anytime by clearing your browser data or using developer tools
  • Deletion Support: Use the in-app "Log Out" button to clear cookies instantly or email fazekas.devon@gmail.com to request manual token revocation, cookie invalidation, or written confirmation that all YNAB-derived data has been purged

6. Your Rights and Controls

  • Local Control: You have complete control over browser storage via your own device settings

  • Data Portability: Since we don't store your budget data, everything remains in your YNAB account unless you export it
  • Deletion Requests: Trigger the built-in "Log Out" action at any time to erase stored tokens, or email fazekas.devon@gmail.com for manual confirmation and revocation
